Max Temperature vs. Latitude


Observations


Unsurprisingly, max temperature is highly correlated with latitude. We find that as latitude approaches the equator (0), temperatures rise accordingly.

An intersting observation is that cities near the equator are much more densely packed and are of a fairly uniform temperature. Whereas both ends of the plot show greater variability in terms of temperature differences between cities of similar latitudes.
