Humidity vs. Latitude


Observations


At first glance, it does not appear that a strong correlation between humidity and latitude exists. However, if you look closely you can see a not insignificant concentration and low variability of humidity around the equator.

The relationship is certainly not as robust as the one between max temperature and latitude, but it exists nonetheless.
