Summary: Latitude and the Weather


The following project utilized the OpenWeatherMap API to extract data and study the relationship between latitude and various atmospheric conditions.


The study analyzed weather data from over 500 cities from a wide range of geographic locations.


Atmospheric properties that we were particularly interestead in were temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. Our resulting dataset and plot visualizations can be found on this website.
